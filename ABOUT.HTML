Our History

Our school was established in 2021 with the mission of providing quality education with values and skills that reinforces critical thinking and innovation by stressing the spritual,moral and intellectual total development of each student. To transform the students into skilled human capital for socio-economic development of the country by ensuring Equitable access to quality education focusing on combating illiteracy,promotion technology,critical thinking and positive values and preparing them in heart and mind, to serve God and the world.
